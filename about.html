About LocumLink

Finding temporary medical staff in Kenya shouldn't rely on endless phone calls and scattered WhatsApp groups. LocumLink is built to change that.

We seamlessly connect understaffed hospitals and clinics with fully verified, ready-to-work locum doctors and nurses. Think of us as a secure marketplace for medical shifts — fast, reliable, and transparent.

Our Mission

To ensure no patient goes unattended due to staffing shortages, while empowering healthcare professionals with flexible, well-paying shifts across Kenya.
